# The Great Nicobar Island Project
The Great Nicobar Project, according to PIB Release 01 MAY 2026, is a strategic project which aims to strengthen India's presence 
in the Andaman sea and Southeast Asia.

*what it means to strengthen India's presence in Andaman sea?*  
*what is the need to strengthen India's presence in Andaman sea?*  
*what is strategic about this location?*

The Project includes  
1. International Container Transhipment Terminal (ICTT) (Capacity-14.2 million TEU)
2. Greenfield International Airport (4000 Peak Hour Passengers-PHP)
3. Power Plant- gas and solar based (450 MVA-Megavolt Ampere)
4. New Township (16610 Hectares)
